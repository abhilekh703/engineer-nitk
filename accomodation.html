Accommodation

Charges

All registered students can avail the accommodation services from 12th October 2018 (evening) to 17th October 2018 (morning).

  • 1 or 2 days - ₹ 1350
  • 3 or 4 days - ₹ 1800
  • 5 or 6 days - ₹ 2200

Policy

  • All registered students must bring their valid college identity card and a passport size photograph, for the insurance of the Accommodation Pass.
  • The check-in time is 11 AM while the check-out time is 10 AM on the following day. Students should strictly vacate the common rooms once the period of their registration ends, failing which a fine of 5000/- will be imposed and their belongings will be put outside the common rooms for which Rendezvous team will not be responsible.
  • Kindly go through the detailed Accommodation Policy.

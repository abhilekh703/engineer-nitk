NITKMUN
Hosted by The Literary, Stage and Debate Society in collaboration with ENGINEER 2018 The first ever inter-college edition of NITKMUN is finally here as part of our college’s tech fest ENGINEER!
Model United Nations (MUN) is a form of diplomatic debating that emulates the policy-making and international politics that goes on in a typical UN session. This year, we’ll be expanding NITKMUN in exciting new ways so we need your skills to make this event a grand success!
The Organising Committee (OC) will be divided to take care of the various aspects of the event as,
  • Volunteers Committee (VC)
  • Executive Board (EB)
  • Awards Committee (AC)
  • Audio and Video Committee (AV)


Once registered, we’ll have an open meet to discuss our plans for the event and the work that needs to be done by each committee.
Note that the deadline for registrations is at 23:59 on September 2nd. Please co-operate, we won’t accept any registrations after that.

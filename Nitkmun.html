NITKMUN
Hosted by The Literary, Stage and Debate Society in collaboration with ENGINEER 2018. The first ever inter-college edition of NITKMUN is finally here as part of our college’s tech fest ENGINEER!
Model United Nations (MUN) is a form of diplomatic debating that emulates the policy-making and international politics that goes on in a typical UN session.
In our third edition of NITKMUN, students can register as,
  • Country Delegates to represent nations and express their foreign policies and draft international policy.
  • Delegates of Journalism to represent news agencies, hold press conferences and express their talents in article writing.
  • Delegates of Media to express their talents in photography and video-making.


There will be several awards for each committee, as well as other exciting prizes to look forward too!
New to MUNing? Don't worry!
We'll also be having a briefing session on the basics of MUN to ensure everyone has all the knowledge necessary to make NITKMUN a great place to start their journey.

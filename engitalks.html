Henry Menezes

Mr. Henry Menezes is a former India national football team goalkeeper, now the Deputy Chairman of All India Football Federation (AIFF) Technical Committee and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) at the Western India Football Association (WIFA) which is the Governing body for football in the state of Maharashtra; affiliated to the All India Football Federation. Also known as one of the "Renaissance Men of Indian Football" Mr. Menezes has been the General Manager of Mahindra United Football Club, Mumbai and was instrumental in creating Mumbai FC. He is a "Shiv Chhatrapati Award" winner, which is the highest Maharashtra State award for excellence in sports.
